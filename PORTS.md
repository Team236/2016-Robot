### DIO
| Port | Subsystem | Assignment
|------|-----------|-----------
| 0 | Drive | Left Encoder, channel A
| 1 | Drive | Left Encoder, channel B
| 2 | Drive | Right Encoder, channel A
| 3 | Drive | Right Encoder, channel B
| 4 | Arm   | Encoder, channel A
| 5 | Arm   | Encoder, channel B
| 6 | Arm   | Upper limit
| 7 | Arm   | Bottom limit
| 8 | Intake | Ball limit
